About the Site

How this works:

  • Players in the current list are required to have played in the last 2 months, as well as having less than 200 RD (about 6-10 games)
  • Every player who has ever played in a tournament has their scores saved in the full rankings list (Ratings start at 1000), but will be excluded from the active list unless they meet the requirements.
  • The RD stands for the Rating Deviation, the higher the RD the more rating gained/lost for each win, in addition to being a good guess of how certain we are of a players rating.
  • Uses a version of the method found on the site this is based off of http://aligulac.com/about/faq/, as well as the python code for Glicko2 found here https://github.com/ryankirkman/pyglicko2

I've included all tournaments after the ESL pre-season, and new tournaments should be updated in the less than a week.
