About the Site

How this works:

  • Every player that has ever played in an Iron Harvest tournament has their games and stats saved, although enough games must be played to get their rating deviation (RD) below 200, this can take anywhere from 5-12 tournament games, once this occurs the player will be permanently added to the full list, and will be included in the active list if they have played in the last two months.
  • The RD stands for the Rating Deviation, the higher the RD the more rating gained/lost for each win, in addition to being a good guess of how certain we are of a players rating.
  • Uses pretty much the same system found on the site this is based off of http://aligulac.com/about/faq/, as well as the python code for Glicko2 found here https://github.com/ryankirkman/pyglicko2

Questions That Could be Asked Frequently:

Why was this site made?

I made this site for multiple reasons, A) I wanted to flesh out the competitive scene in Iron Harvest and figure out who really was the best, and B) I thought that this would be a cool project, and after finding out how http://aligulac.com makes its data and the fact that that code was free to use, I set about implementing it in python and slowly working from a bunch of .txts in a folder to eventually a database on a website.

What tournaments are included in the data?

I've included all tournaments after the ESL pre-season, and new tournaments should be updated in the less than a week after they happen.

What's next for the site?

  • A better system to display past matches as well as archives for previous ranking list
  • A better navigation and player profiles with more data
  • Code improvements and optimizations that you won't notice but will take up my time
  • Prediction capabilities
